Area Reference

Western Isintris (Bad-Lands)

Description
A desert wasteland home to a city of thievery, debauchery, and scandal. There are rumors of the remaining Quazars roaming here.

Eastern Isintris (Wolf's Den)

Description
An arctic landscape that acts as the White Fang's headquarters, the Wolf's Den. Suspicious reports say the White Fang have been quiet as of late.

Central Isintris (Nirvana Falls)

Description
This hidden area of Central Isintris protrudes an air of tranquility. Nirvana Falls is the perfect area to meditate or find oneself. However, visitors must be weary of violating village law.
