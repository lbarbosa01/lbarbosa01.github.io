Campaign: The Lost Lore

Central Isintris (Nirvana Falls)

Description
This hidden area of Central Isintris protrudes an air of tranquility. Nirvana Falls is the perfect area to meditate or find oneself. However, visitors must be weary of violating village law.

The Docks

Description
A lively area with courteous neighbors. Most citizens of Nirvana Falls live in The Docks, and many have cultivated different hobbies to pass the time in the small village.

Stone of the Ancients

Description
The great stone pillar where the founders of Nirvana Falls rest. It's forbidden to enter this tomb: the last person who wondered in was never seen again.

The Everspring

Description
The greatest treasure of Nirvana Falls. Tales of enlightenment, healing, and miracles originate here. It's illegal to harm wild-life or desecrate the area in any way.

Headmaster BernSan

Description Status
His lack of personality and indifference to everyone makes him the least popular Headmaster of the Falls. He is in charge of documentation and administration. Alive

Headmaster MaGa

Description Status
The most popular Headmaster of Nirvana Falls, mostly because of his leadership and demeanor. He is mostly in charge of business and economic policy. His position is arguably the most powerful of the Headmasters. Dead

Headmaster DenBi

Description Status
The arch-rival of Headmaster MaGa. He's been gaining traction as of late due to the strange events of Nirvana Falls. Alive

Headmaster KamHala

Description Status
The Headmaster of domestic affairs and environmental policy. Citizens tread carefully around her since she's the strictest and most judgemental of the Headmasters despite her beauty. Alive

Ivanka

Description Status
The pride and joy of Headmaster MaGa. She's always off adventuring in nature while her father works. Alive
